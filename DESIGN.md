# Design Direction: BotSitePro

## Style: Dark & Premium
- **Background**: White (Primary content area) / Dark (Accents/Sections) -> *Correction from prompt:* "Style: [тёмный], Background: [белый]". I will interpret this as a light-themed landing page with dark, premium accents (graphite) and a very "clean" look.
- **Accents**: Grey Graphite (#333533 or similar)
- **Typography**:
  - **Headlines**: Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Source Serif 4 via Google Fonts)
  - **Body**: Clean Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter or Roboto via Google Fonts)

## Aesthetic Principles
- **Vibrant & Premium**: Use sophisticated shadows and subtle gradients.
- **Glassmorphism**: Apply to fixed nav or cards for a modern feel.
- **Dynamic**: Micro-animations on hover and scroll.
- **Visual Excellence**: Avoid basic colors; use harmonious graphite and off-white tones.

## Components
- **Navbar**: Minimalist, sticky, glassmorphism.
- **Hero**: Bold serif headline, clear CTA.
- **Services**: Card-based with icons and clear ROI messaging.
- **Team**: Circular avatars, clean labels.
- **Testimonials**: Elegant quote blocks.
- **Form**: Floating labels, premium inputs.
